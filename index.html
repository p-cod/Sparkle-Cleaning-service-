Our Services

Everything We Do For You

Six comprehensive cleaning services tailored to every situation — from post-construction deep dives to gentle weekly routines.

✨

Deep Cleaning

Our most comprehensive clean — ideal for homes that haven't been professionally cleaned in a while. We scrub, sanitise, and detail every corner of your home including behind appliances, inside cabinets, grout lines, ceiling fans, and more. Perfect for seasonal refreshes, pre-event prep, or before settling into a new rhythm.

🏗️

Post Construction Cleaning

Construction leaves behind concrete dust, paint splatters, wood chips, and debris — we handle all of it. Our team uses specialised equipment and techniques to transform a newly built structure into a clean, safe, and habitable space. Ideal for both residential and commercial builds.

🎨

Post Renovation Cleaning

Renovation work — tiling, painting, plastering — leaves a layer of mess that's hard to clean on your own. We come in after the contractors leave and make your renovated space shine. Every surface is wiped down, every speck of dust removed, and the space is left looking like a showroom.

🏠

Move In Cleaning

Moving into a new space should feel exciting, not stressful. We deep clean your new home before your belongings arrive so you can start fresh in a spotless environment. Covers all rooms, bathrooms, kitchen, and storage areas — sanitised and ready for you to call it home.

📅

Routine Cleaning

Stay on top of cleanliness without lifting a finger. Our routine cleaning visits keep your home consistently clean — dust-free, mopped, surfaces wiped, and bathrooms sanitised. Choose weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly visits. Special student-friendly weekly packages available at discounted rates.

👕

Laundry Services

From bedsheets to delicates, we handle your laundry with care. Clothes are washed, dried, and neatly folded (or ironed on request). We treat every fabric carefully, using the right products for each type. Drop off your laundry or combine with a cleaning appointment for convenience.

Not sure which service you need?

Just send us a message and describe your space. We'll recommend the right service and give you a quote — no obligation.

About Us

We Clean With Care

Sparkle Cleaning Service was founded on one simple belief — every home deserves to shine. We bring professional standards to your doorstep with warmth and reliability.

Our Story

Born From a Passion for Clean Living

Sparkle Cleaning Service started as a neighborhood initiative — a small team with mops, gloves, and a genuine love for clean spaces. Word spread quickly. Clients saw the difference a truly thorough clean makes: not just visually, but how it feels to live and breathe in a fresh environment.

Today, we've expanded our team and services while holding tightly to our founding values: trustworthiness, thoroughness, and genuine care for every client we serve.

From deep cleans to post-construction overhauls, from student accommodations to family homes — we show up ready to make it sparkle.
